# Anti-Patterns

## Do not evaluate templates only by screenshot beauty

A pretty screenshot does not guarantee reusable structure.

## Do not copy old template code blindly

Legacy jQuery, outdated plugins, and browser hacks should not be imported without review.

## Do not keep every template at equal priority

A pattern library becomes noisy if representative samples are not selected.

## Do not confuse decoration with information architecture

The most reusable value often comes from layout flow and section order, not ornamental effects.
