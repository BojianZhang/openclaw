# Sample Selection

Use this file when choosing representative templates from a large collection.

## Goal

Do not keep every template equal. Select a smaller set of representative samples that cover the main structure families.

## What makes a strong sample

- clear primary use case
- reusable section structure
- enough page variety to learn from
- not too broken or incomplete
- not purely decorative without structural value

## Suggested selection logic

Pick samples that represent:
- the most common site types
- the most reusable layouts
- the strongest examples of a specific niche, such as photography or medical
- a mix of light templates and heavier multi-page templates

## Warning

Do not let the noisiest or most bloated template dominate the library if it does not actually teach reusable patterns.
